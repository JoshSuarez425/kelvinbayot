Learning in the Modern IT Era

Our Learning Experiences

2022 - 2026

Online Classes

We experienced online classes during the pandemic, which was a new and challenging way of learning. We had to adapt to using different platforms and tools, and we had to find ways to stay motivated and focused. However, we also learned how to be more independent and resourceful, and we were able to connect with our classmates and teachers in new ways.

2024 - 2026

Learning Apps

We encountered learning apps like chatgpt and quizlet, which were helpful and convenient tools for studying and practicing. We could use them anytime and anywhere, and they provided us with instant feedback and personalized recommendations

2024 - 2026

E-books

We used e-books to review for our exams, which were more accessible and interactive than traditional textbooks. We could highlight and annotate the text, and we could also access additional resources and multimedia content

2020 - 2026

Research On The internet

Even before Senior HighSchool researching for stuff in the internet was already a common thing for us, but it became more important and efficient during our college years. We could find information and answers to our questions in seconds, and we could also access a wide range of sources and perspectives. However, we also had to be careful and critical about the quality and reliability of the information we found online.

Online Communication Methods

  • All
  • Digital Communication
  • Messaging Applications
  • Collaboration

Social Media Platforms

Social media helps people share information, photos,
and updates with friends and family around the world.
Platforms like Facebook and Instagram allow users to stay connected anytime.

Messaging Applications

Messaging apps allow instant communication through text messages, voice notes, and file sharing. They make communication faster and more convenient.

Video Call Technology

Video calls allow people to communicate face-to-face even if they are far away. This is commonly used for online classes, meetings, and connecting with family.

Email Communication

Email is a professional way of sending messages, documents, and important information through the internet. It is widely used in schools and workplaces.

Mobile Communication Apps

Mobile apps make communication easier through smartphones. People can send messages, share photos, and make calls anywhere using internet connection.

Digital Collaboration Tools

Online collaboration tools allow teams and students to work together remotely. These platforms help people communicate, share ideas, and complete projects efficiently.
